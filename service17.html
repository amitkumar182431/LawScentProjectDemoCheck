About Court Marriage & Marriage Registration

The Court Marriage & Marriage Registration practice at LawScent simplifies one of the most fundamental civil rights — the legal recognition of marriage. Governed primarily by the Special Marriage Act, 1954, and supported by respective personal laws, the process formalizes a union before the state, ensuring legal validity and social security.

Legal Framework

Expert guidance under Special Marriage Act, 1954 and respective personal laws.

Process Management

Comprehensive handling from notice and verification to solemnization and registration.

Secular Protection

Ensuring legal validity beyond religious or social barriers under secular law.

Constitutional Values

Court marriage represents more than procedure — it embodies the constitutional values of choice, equality, and liberty. It provides individuals with the right to marry beyond religious or social barriers and ensures protection under secular law.

This practice covers every stage of the process — from notice and verification to solemnization and registration — ensuring that the requirements under local authorities and statutory timelines are fulfilled seamlessly.

Our Approach

LawScent's approach emphasizes clarity and confidentiality, safeguarding the rights and privacy of couples while ensuring strict adherence to procedural and legal formalities.

In a society where personal freedom often meets institutional complexity, this practice ensures that love, when lawful, remains protected by both conscience and constitution.
