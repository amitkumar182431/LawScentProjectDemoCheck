About Employment & Salary Disputes

Employment and salary disputes represent the intersection of law, fairness, and workplace accountability. The Employment & Salary Disputes practice at LawScent deals with matters involving unpaid wages, wrongful termination, breach of employment contracts, and violations of statutory entitlements.

The legal structure governing these issues derives from the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, Payment of Wages Act, 1936, and Shops & Establishment Acts, alongside principles of contract and labour law.
