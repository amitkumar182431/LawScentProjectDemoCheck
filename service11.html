About Banking & Finance

The Banking & Finance practice at LawScent anchors the firm's commercial advisory framework. It deals with the structuring, negotiation, and regulation of financial transactions that drive the economy. This domain encompasses corporate lending, project finance, securitization, asset reconstruction, and debt restructuring under frameworks such as the Banking Regulation Act, 1949, SARFAESI Act, 2002, and RBI Master Directions.

Corporate Lending

Expert structuring and documentation of corporate loan facilities and credit arrangements.

Project Finance

Specialized advisory for infrastructure and large-scale project financing structures.

Debt Restructuring

Strategic solutions for debt recovery, asset reconstruction, and financial rehabilitation.

Modern Financial Challenges

The financial sector's complexity today demands not just legal precision but commercial insight. Financial arrangements must withstand regulatory scrutiny, economic volatility, and cross-border challenges.

Banking law is also rapidly converging with technology — from digital lending and fintech compliance to data security and payment regulations.

Legal Philosophy

At its essence, this practice represents balance — between risk and reward, innovation and regulation, capital and compliance. LawScent's approach to banking and finance is founded on prudence, clarity, and the understanding that every transaction shapes confidence in the system itself.
