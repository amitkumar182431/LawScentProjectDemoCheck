About Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)

In a rapidly evolving global economy, intellectual property is the most powerful form of capital. It defines creativity, innovation, and market identity. The Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) practice at LawScent is dedicated to the protection, management, and enforcement of these intangible assets, which now shape corporate value and competitive advantage.

The Indian IP framework — under the Trade Marks Act, Patents Act, Copyright Act, and Designs Act — has grown into a dynamic system influenced by global treaties such as TRIPS and the Madrid Protocol. Our practice interprets these laws not just as statutory obligations but as strategic instruments that secure commercial vision and foster innovation.

Complete IP Lifecycle Management

This area encompasses the entire IP lifecycle — registration, prosecution, opposition, enforcement, portfolio management, licensing, and cross-border transactions. IPR is no longer isolated from commerce; it is integrated into brand valuation, mergers and acquisitions, franchising, and due diligence processes. The discipline requires foresight to identify potential risks, adaptability to technological change, and precision in execution.

LawScent's philosophy toward IPR reflects the balance between creativity and protection. Intellectual property is more than a legal right — it is an expression of trust between innovators, markets, and law. Protecting it means protecting the very foundation of progress.
