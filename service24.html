About Media & Telecommunications

The Media & Telecommunications practice at LawScent focuses on the complex regulatory environment governing communication, broadcasting, and connectivity in the digital age. It brings together the disciplines of technology law, data governance, and infrastructure regulation to support enterprises operating at the frontier of communication innovation.

Telecom Licensing

Expert guidance on licensing under Telegraph Act, TRAI compliance, and spectrum management.

Digital Infrastructure

Advisory on 5G networks, IoT, cloud communications, and broadband infrastructure development.

Regulatory Compliance

Comprehensive compliance advisory for ISPs, broadcasters, and digital communication platforms.

Comprehensive Telecom Services

This practice spans the entire telecom ecosystem — licensing under the Telegraph Act, 1885, compliance with TRAI guidelines, spectrum management, interconnection agreements, and regulatory advisory for telecom service providers, ISPs, and digital broadcasters. It also extends to satellite communication, broadband infrastructure, and emerging technologies such as 5G networks, IoT, and cloud-based communication systems.

In the age of convergence, media and telecom are no longer distinct industries — they are two sides of the same digital economy. The practice also integrates advisory on digital data transmission, cybersecurity, intermediary liability, and content regulation under the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021.

Legal Philosophy

LawScent's telecom practice blends legal understanding with technical fluency — ensuring that clients remain compliant, competitive, and prepared for the future. The emphasis lies on strategic licensing, dispute resolution with regulators, and advisory on technology-driven collaborations between telecom operators and digital content platforms.

Telecommunications law is more than regulatory compliance — it is the backbone of connectivity, governance, and national infrastructure. LawScent's work in this domain strengthens the trust that underpins every signal, every broadcast, and every connection — sustaining progress in an era defined by communication.
