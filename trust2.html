Compliance & Regulatory Services

Stay compliant with ever-changing regulations. Our team ensures your business meets all legal requirements, handles regulatory filings, and maintains proper documentation to protect your interests and avoid penalties.

Why Compliance Matters

In today's complex regulatory environment, non-compliance can result in severe penalties, reputational damage, and operational disruptions. Our proactive compliance solutions help you navigate regulatory frameworks with confidence, ensuring your business remains protected and penalty-free while focusing on growth.

Corporate Compliance

Ensure your company adheres to all statutory requirements under the Companies Act, 2013, and related regulations.

  • Annual Return (MGT-7) and Financial Statement filing (AOC-4)
  • Board and General Meeting compliances
  • Director KYC (DIR-3 KYC) and appointment formalities
  • Maintenance of statutory registers and records
  • Share capital and dividend compliance
  • Related party transaction disclosures

Regulatory Advisory

Navigate complex regulatory frameworks with expert guidance tailored to your business operations.

  • SEBI regulations for listed companies and securities
  • RBI guidelines for banking and finance
  • FEMA compliance for foreign exchange transactions
  • Competition Commission of India (CCI) filings
  • Data protection and privacy laws (DPDP Act)
  • Industry-specific regulatory requirements

Statutory Filings & Documentation

Timely and accurate filing of all mandatory forms and documents with regulatory authorities.

  • ROC filings (e-Forms filing with MCA)
  • GST returns and compliance
  • Income tax and TDS compliances
  • PF, ESI, and labor law filings
  • FSSAI, trade license, and other registrations
  • Annual compliance calendar management

Compliance Audits

Comprehensive audits to identify compliance gaps and ensure adherence to legal requirements.

  • Secretarial audit under Companies Act
  • Internal compliance reviews
  • Regulatory compliance assessment
  • Gap analysis and remediation plans
  • Due diligence for M&A transactions
  • Post-acquisition compliance integration

Sector-Specific Compliance

Specialized compliance services for regulated industries with unique requirements.

  • Banking and financial services (RBI, SEBI)
  • Healthcare and pharmaceuticals (FDA, CDSCO)
  • Education institutions (UGC, AICTE)
  • Real estate and construction (RERA)
  • E-commerce and digital platforms
  • Manufacturing and industrial units

Corporate Governance & Risk Management

Strengthen your governance framework to minimize compliance risks and enhance organizational integrity.

  • Board governance and best practices
  • Policy development (code of conduct, ethics, whistle-blower)
  • Risk identification and mitigation strategies
  • Compliance committee establishment
  • ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) compliance
  • Anti-corruption and anti-bribery measures

Compliance Training & Awareness

Empower your team with knowledge and tools to maintain compliance culture across the organization.

  • Executive and board-level compliance workshops
  • Employee compliance training programs
  • Regulatory update sessions
  • Department-specific compliance modules
  • Compliance manual development
  • Internal compliance champion programs

Ongoing Monitoring & Regulatory Updates

Stay ahead of regulatory changes with continuous monitoring and timely advisory.

  • Regular compliance health checks
  • Regulatory change alerts and impact analysis
  • Quarterly compliance reviews
  • Proactive deadline management
  • Government notification tracking
  • Strategic advisory for new regulations
