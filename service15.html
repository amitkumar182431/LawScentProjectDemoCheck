About Arbitration & ADR

The global business environment increasingly demands resolution mechanisms that are swift, confidential, and commercially sensible. The Arbitration and Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) practice at LawScent is designed to meet this demand — combining legal expertise with strategic pragmatism.

Arbitration has emerged as the preferred method for resolving commercial disputes, governed by the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, which aligns with international standards under the UNCITRAL Model Law.

Comprehensive ADR Services

The practice extends across domestic and international arbitration, institutional and ad hoc proceedings, mediation, and conciliation. Our approach to arbitration is rooted in meticulous drafting of dispute resolution clauses, careful case analysis, and persuasive advocacy before arbitral tribunals.

We also assist clients in enforcing and challenging arbitral awards before Indian courts, ensuring that procedural integrity and commercial outcomes remain in balance.

Strategic Resolution Approach

Alternative dispute resolution is not limited to arbitration — it includes negotiation and mediation frameworks that preserve relationships while achieving effective closure. In today's fast-paced economy, ADR represents a strategic shift from confrontation to collaboration.

LawScent's arbitration practice spans diverse sectors, including construction, energy, infrastructure, finance, technology, and joint venture disputes. Each engagement reflects a balance between confidentiality, speed, and fairness.

Legal Philosophy

At its core, arbitration is not a shortcut to justice — it is an evolution of it. It represents the fusion of law and diplomacy, where results are achieved through reasoning rather than resistance.

Through this practice, LawScent continues to strengthen its commitment to intelligent dispute resolution — Protecting What Matters while preserving the principles of trust, efficiency, and equity.
